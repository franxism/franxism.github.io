MULTIPAC GP32 (27/11/2004)


 Port of Multipac for the GP32 console.

 It emulates arcades with Pac Man hardware: Pac Man, Ms Pac Man, Crush Roller and all clones or hacks of previous games.

 It was done as a contribution to ADIC 2004 Compo (December, 2004).
